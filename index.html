INTRODUCTION

Tired of getting lost in AUP? Well fear no more! Our team will make your life a little bit easier. By using this AUP app YOU will never get lost again!


ON EVER ONWARD MY DEAR AUPIANS! THIS APP WILL FIND YOUR WAYS!

These are the features that you might like in the coming release

Hyperlocal Navigation

Go anywhere inside AUP and always find the exact place where you need to go. AVN provides you with the most detailed map of every location inside the campus. Whether it's your first day finding your class room or it's your first time in the campus, you will never be lost with AVN.

Step by Step Navigation

With a Google Maps powered navigation experience, expect to be in the right place without stressing about the accuracy of the directions. Every street, roads, buildings, rooms, and halls are precisely mapped for your convenience.

Dining Suggestions

Looking for a quick snack, dinner or whatever craving you have? AVN will suggest a dining spot fit for you based on your preferences and proximity. Just open the app and switch to Dining Mode and you'll quickly see which spots are available.

Wi-Fi and Mobile Data Radar

Need reliable internet connection badly? The app's got you covered. The app will guide you to spots where Eastern iConnect or strong LTE/3G signals are available.

Event Tracking

Got some time to unwind, watch some games or attend interesting events? Just open the AVN app to get informed of events near you and events that will take place soon.

Location Diaries

Write about your most memorable stories in a certain spot inside the campus and store up happy memories while you stay here. You can share your stories anonymously too.
